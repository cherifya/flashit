Flash It!


This is a bookmarklet (javascript bookmark) that generates a flashcode to let you load the page you are reading on your desktop on your smartphone.

To quickly and easily send content you encounter online to your smartphone, drag the Flash It! button below to your browser's bookmarks toolbar. Then whenever you'd like to send the page you're reading to your Kindle, click the bookmarklet.
